Whether you're recording an album, composing a soundtrack, or trying to reproduce an effect for a video, it can often be difficult to find the right sound. Studios have banks of synthesizers, costing thousands of dollars, but studio time is costly. What if you could choose from a selection of instruments and have the audio sent to you for a fraction of the cost of buying the hardware? That's SynthShare!

As a user of SynthShare, you can browse all the available hardware that has been added to our system. You can select a portion of your score, and send it over. You can listen to a preview version for free, and when you've got the perfect sound, you can ask for the final version and send a micropayment.

If you have music hardware, you can agree to provide some of your kit online for our users: you can specify when it should be available, and even how much you charge for usage. If your studio is only open until 8pm, you can hire out you equipment right up until you reopen in the morning.

Your donation will go towards the development of the SynthShare audio plugins and hardware sharing software. The plugins will be designed to be compatible with the major music production packages, though more funding will let us add more support. It will also let us purchase sound hardware, so we can build technology needed to interface with it, and also let us provide some hardware to users from launch.

FAQ

How will I connect my hardware?
The SynthShare Box will have MIDI in/out connectors, an audio input jack, and an ethernet port. Simply connect the MIDI connectors up to your synth, the audio output to the input jack, and then plug the box into your router.
